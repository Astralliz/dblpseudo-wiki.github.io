MAIN ABILITY

Warrior Blood

Restores own health by 30% and Ki by 40.

+30% to damage inflicted (cannot be cancelled).

Increases own Arts Card Draw Speed by 1 level (cannot be cancelled).

+25% to damage inflicted by allies (cannot be cancelled).

+15% to damage inflicted by allied "Episode: Game Originals" (cannot be cancelled).

Shortens allies' substitution counts by 3.

Requirements: 10 timer counts must elapse.

UNIQUE ABILITY

Burning Power of Instinct

Applies the following effects to self when battle starts:

+80% to damage inflicted (cannot be cancelled).

Reduces damage received by 60% (cannot be cancelled).

Applies the following effects to self when this character enters the battlefield:

Randomly draws 1 new card when you have 3 or fewer cards.

Restores Ki by 20.

+20 to Strike damage inflicted for 15 timer counts.

Also applies the following effects to self if there is a defeated battle member:

Restores health by 15%.

+70% to damage inflicted.

+70% to Ki Recovery.

Effects reset after character switch or transformation.

Heart Filled with Faith

Applies the following effects to self once 3 timer counts have elapsed after entering the battlefield:

Restores Ki by 30.

+60% to damage inflicted.

Effects reset after character switch or transformation.

Knocks enemy back to long range if a cover change is performed against their Strike Arts attack (activates during assists).

[Comboable Arts]

Special Move Arts (excluding some such as wide-range attacks, etc.)

The following effects occur when changing cover:

Randomly destroys one of your own Cards and draws a Special Move Arts Card next (activates once).

Reduces damage received by 10% until combo ends.

Restores allied "Episode: Game Originals" health by 5% (activates four times).

Restores 40% of damage received when changing cover.
